Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes! You can change your plan anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades apply from the next billing cycle.

Q: Is there a long-term contract?

No long-term contracts required! All plans are month-to-month. However, we offer discounts for 6-month and annual commitments.

Q: What if I need more posts than my plan includes?

No problem! You can purchase additional posts at Rs 500 per post, or consider upgrading to the next tier for better value.

Q: Do you offer custom packages?

Absolutely! If our standard plans don't fit your needs, we'll create a custom package tailored specifically for your business.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, eSewa, Khalti, credit/debit cards, and digital wallets. Payment is collected at the start of each month.

Q: Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees! Your first month's payment covers everything including initial strategy sessions and account setup.
